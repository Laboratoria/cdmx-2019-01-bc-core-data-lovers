Motorcycles are a fast and seemingly efficient form of personal transportation. They differ from other motor vehicles in their physical operation, much smaller size (and lower weight), two heel (rather than four-wheel) base, and driver exposure (with seating on top of the vehicle rather than inside it). Their design renders them highly unstable for many potential users while providing little protection in the event of a crash.
